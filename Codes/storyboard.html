List of Web Pages and Their Purpose

  • Home Page: The home page is to introduce viewers to my website and what it entails.
  • Professional Page: The professional page is to display my resume, aspired career, and overall professional life.
  • Personality Page: The personality page showcases my personality traits, hobbies, interests, and introduces a person I look up to.
  • Development Page: The Personal Development or "Development" Page summarizes and reflects on the book "Twelve Pillars" by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener and also highlights my goals for personal development.
  • Storyboard Page: The storyboard page shows the goal I had in mind for the layout and theme for my website.
  • References Page: The reference page lists all of my sources.

Theme of the Site

The theme of my website aims to reflect simplicity, professionalism and elegance.

  • Color Scheme:
    • Primary Colors: #330000
    • Secondary Color: #000000
    • Accent Color: #660000 and #ffffff
  • Graphics: Modern and minimalistic icons and images.
  • Target Audience: Potential employers and colleagues.

List of Features

  • Images: A collection of images displays what is being explained by words on each page.
  • Videos: Videos illustrate the text on pages.
