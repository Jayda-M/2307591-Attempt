Future Career: Data Analyst

Skills


  • Proficiency in programming languages especially: Python, R, SQL

  • Strong understanding of statistical methods and data modeling. Data analysts are expected to be proficient in foundational statistical methodologies as well as current SAS (a statistical software suite) technologies. Statistical analysis is core to the work of data analysts and used extensively in predictive analytics.

  • Experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI). Data visualization helps data analysts clearly display data and translate insights for a nontechnical audience. Tableau is one of the prominent data visualization tools used in industry today, making it a core competency for data analysts.

  • Excellent problem-solving and critical-thinking abilities

  • Excellent problem-solving and critical-thinking abilities- Possessing data preparation abilities allows you to arrange and utilize huge quantities of unstructured data into smaller portions.

  • Strong presenting and communication abilities - Data analysts must clearly convey their findings—whether it’s to an audience of readers or a small team of executives making business decisions.

  • Proficiency in applied machine learning- This is becoming a highly valued skill for data analysts. Applied machine learning can help data analysts quickly extract valuable insights from their data by automatically identifying patterns and trends in the data.

Job Requirements and Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in computer science, statistics, or information systems
  • Certifications in data analysis tools and methodologies
  • Hands-on experience with data projects and internships
  • Ability to work with large datasets and derive insights
  • Proficiency in data visualization and reporting
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
